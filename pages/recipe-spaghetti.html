Spaghetti

    Ingredients

    • Pasta (& water to boil)
    • Tomato Sauce
    • Diced Tomatoes
    • Green Beans
    • Celery
    • Carrots
    • Onions
    • Mushroom
    • Beef
    • Garlic
    • Chili Powder
    • Basil

    Instructions

    This recipe is made up of three sections that can be done simultaneously

    Make Sauce

    1. Chop the veggies
    2. Simmer the carrots and celery in a little bit of water on high heat
    3. Pour in tomato sauce, basil, and the rest of the veggies (except onion, garlic, and mushroom)
    4. Leave to cook on low heat, stir frequently

    Boil the pasta

    1. Boil a large pot of water on high heat
    2. Add pasta and stir frequently until it is soft to the bite

    Cook the meat

    1. Put the ground beef in a pan on medium heat
    2. Add mushrooms, garlic, onion, and chili powder to the pan
    3. Cook until the beef is mostly finished cooking
    4. Add the cooked beef to the sauce as it cooks

    Once the meat is in the sauce, the carrots are soft, and the pasta is done, serve.
