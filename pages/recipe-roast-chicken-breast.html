Roast Chicken Breast

  • Recipes
    •

    Ingredients

    Brine

    • Water (1-2 litres)
    • Salt (about 1/6th to 1/4th of a cup)
    • Pepper (1/8th cup)
    • Lemon (1 whole)
    • Honey (1/4th cup)
    • Parsley (1/8th cup)
    • Thyme (1/8th cup)
    • Rosemary (1/8th cup)
    • garlic (6 cloves)
    • Bay Leaves (2-4 leaves)

    Roast

    • Chicken Breasts
    • Carrots
    • Onions
    • Celery
    • Broccoli
    • Olive Oil (1/4th cup)
    • Seasonings

    Instructions

    Make the Brine

    1. Put 1 litre of water on heat to boil
    2. Chop the lemon into 8 pieces
    3. Add the lemon chunks to the water as it heats, squeeze each chunk to let the juice out
    4. Add about 1/4th to 1/8th cups of salt to the water
    5. Add pepper, honey, parsley, thyme, rosemary, and garlic to the water
    6. As soon as the mixture starts to boil, remove from heat and let sit for 2-3 hours until it is cool to the touch

    Brine the Chicken

    1. Cut the chicken breast into thinner chunks, to improve brining and cooking
    2. Fully immerse the chicken breast chunks within the cold brine
    3. IMPORTANT: DO NOT soak chicken in warm brine, this will create harmful bacteria
    4. Place the brining chicken in the fridge for up to 24 hours
    5. Once finished brining, pour the brine out and place the chicken back in the fridge until you are ready to cook it

    Roast the Chicken

    1. Preheat the oven to 400F
    2. Chop the vegetables
    3. Simmer carrots, celery, and broccoli to soften them a bit
    4. Put a thin layer of oil on a cooking tray
    5. Place the brined chicken on the tray, and fill the surrounding area with chopped veggies
    6. Add seasonings to everything on the tray
    7. Place the filled tray in the oven and cook for 37 minutes
    8. Check to make sure the chicken has fully cooked through
